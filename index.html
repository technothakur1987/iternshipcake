Dear Thakur’s Bakery Community,

First and foremost, I hope you and your family are in good health.

It has been an unbelievable and unprecedented year and a half. My sisters, son, nieces, nephews, and our incredible team have been working tirelessly in our bakeries trying to continue to take care of our guests. During this time of profound uncertainty, which no words will do justice, the Thakurs’s team has made all efforts to adapt to the constantly evolving situation.

We are excited that our communities are getting safer. All locations are now open. Accordingly, we will be beginning to limit our pickup availability in order to properly accommodate our dine-in guests. If you have any trouble placing an online order, you are welcome to come dine-in with us!

Thank you for your kindness and patience as our team continues to work hard to serve our guests and communities. In speaking with our guests these past few months, I’ve come to realize that we’re not just feeding the community, but we’re providing a sense of comfort and normalcy to their homes during these uncertain times

We are all in this together! Be safe and we look forward to continuing to serve you.

Sincerely,

Thakur Singh

and the entire Thakur’s Bakery

family and team
